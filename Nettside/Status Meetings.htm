11:20 AM

What is a Status Meeting?
    • Status meeting is intended to be a review of projects where risk and issues are discussed and appropriate action is taken regarding those issues
      • Attendees should have a general understanding of overall project status prior to the meeting

Schedule appropriately

Set a meeting that allows for travel time for participants, For Example:

  • 45 minutes instead of an hour meeting
  • 22 minutes instead of a half hour meeting

Write the meeting invite

In Outlook:

  • Use the Status Meeting Invite 
  • Add a LYNC meeting
  • Add OneNote Meeting Notes

During the Meeting
    • Use the Status Meeting Notes template
    • In OneNote, Add the Meeting Details via the Home tab

Follow-Up

After the meeting, send a follow-up email with Notes link, Action Items and Key Takeaways

 

Opprettet med OneNote. Den vedlagte OneNote-filen (.one) kan brukes for å legge til notatene i din egen OneNote.
