What is a Meeting One-pager?

A generalized, compacted Meeting Notes Template for ad-hoc meetings and 1:1s

Schedule appropriately

Set a meeting that allows for travel time for participants.

For Example:

  • 45 minutes instead of an hour meeting
  • 22 minutes instead of a half hour meeting

Write the meeting invite

In Outlook:

  • Provide a basic agenda in the invite
    • This will also be include in your OneNote notes
  • Add a LYNC meeting
  • Add OneNote Meeting Notes

During the Meeting
    • Use the One Pager template
    • In OneNote, Add the Meeting Details via the Home tab

Follow-Up

After the meeting, send a follow-up email with Notes link, Action Items and Key Takeaways
